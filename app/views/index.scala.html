Gladiators - The Game

Now available for Browsers

Gladiators is a turn-based strategy game for 2 Players. Players can buy different Gladiators (such as Knights, Archer or Tanks) for a certain amount of money (credits). Money can get earned by killing enemy gladiators or attacking the gold mine that is randomly positioned on the board. Gladiators can be bought in the shop. They have a set of attributes that specify their skills. Attributes are:

  • Movement Points (how far can a Gladiator walk per turn)
  • Health Points (how much damage can a Gladiator take until it dies)
  • Attach Points (how much damage can a Gladiator make per attack)

The shop generates random gladiators and provides them to the players. Each Gladiator-Type has a possible interval of values for each attribute.

Gladiator-Type Movement-Points Health-Points Attach-Points
Knight 3 - 4 50 - 80 50 - 80
Archer 2 - 3 30 - 50 40 - 70
Tank 1 - 2 80 - 100 30 - 50

The goal of the game is to destroy the enemy's base by attacking it.
