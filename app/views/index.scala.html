Gladiators - The Game

Now available for Browsers

Gladiators is a turn-based strategy game for 2 Players. Players can buy different Gladiators (such as Knights, Archer or Tanks) for different amount of credits. Each Gladiator has attributes which specify its skills. Attributes are:

  • Movement Points (how far gan a Gladiator walk per turn)
  • Health Points (how much damage can a Gladiator take until it dies)
  • Attach Points (how much damage can a Gladiator make per attack)

Gladiators can be bought in the shop. Each Gladiator-Type has a possible interval of values for each attribute. They get randomly-generated.

Gladiator-Type Movement-Points Health-Points Attach-Points
Knight 3 - 4 50 - 80 50 - 80
Archer 2 - 3 30 - 50 40 - 70
Tank 1 - 2 80 - 100 30 - 50
